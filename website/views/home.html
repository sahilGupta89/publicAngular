Who We Are

Sterling Pixels provides access to a 360o learning solution for preschool and K-12 grades to our customers around the world. Our videos and eBooks are supported by the knowledge and experience of experts in the fields of replica Science, Maths and Earth science, and allow users to learn at their own pace.

Our Aims

  • To provide educational products tailored to our customers' needs around the world
  • To partner with schools, educational institutions, and government education departments to make the contemporary learning experience more engaging
  • To provide easily accessible online education to students in schools, and to home learners
  • To provide resource material for teachers to aid teaching

Sterling Pixels' user base is spread across 16 countries and is increasing rapidly.

E-Safety Tips

Say no to cyber bullying. Use technology safely, positively and respectfully.

Think before posting anything hurtful or embarrassing, and before opening suspicious e-mails.

Respect others’ copyrights, and take proper permissions before using someone else’s work.

Do not share your personal information with strangers online or on suspicious websites.

Tell an adult if you are worried about or uncomfortable with something or someone online.

Be discerning. Filter out spam, false information and suspicious persons online.
